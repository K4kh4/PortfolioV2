Lidl
Pfand ID

A great portion of homeless in Germany rely on collecting refundable bottles to survive. But their work goes unnoticed. Pfand ID turns bottle collection into a verified work record, helping collectors access jobs, training, and dignity through a system they already use.

Launched with LIDL and social partners, the campaign doubles bottle refunds for registered collectors and prints real-time "resumes" at Pfand machines. It reframes recycling as labor, making their invisible work visible, bottle by bottle.
